About Me

I am a native Washingtonian, born and raised in the San Juan Islands, and a graduate of the University of Washington.

After spending 4 years as a stay-at-home father I started self-training for a career in web development. I spent a year learning front-end technologies and then attended an online bootcamp through Coding Dojo to learn full-stack development in Node.js and Python.

I love the freedom and creativity of coding and the endless opportunities to learn new skills and technologies.

I enjoy hiking, kayaking and birdwatching with my family. I am also passionate about wildlife conservation and I have worked for years as a volunteer with several conservation organizations in Africa.
